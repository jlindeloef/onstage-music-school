About us

We who work at Onstage music school are dedicated teachers who want to share the joy of music together with you and lead you through a journey of everything that music has to offer.

The teachers are active musicians who play in different constellations ranging from small bands to big orchestras with various music genres.

We hope that you too will share your passion for music with others. So no matter what instrument you want to learn to play, we will do everything we can to help you in your development!
