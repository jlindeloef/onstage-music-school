Onstage Music School

About us

Onstage music school are a place for learning, playing and creating music and mainly reaches out for youths between 5-19 years old. We are dedicaded and want to share the joy of music together with you and lead you through a journey of everything that music has to offer. The teachers are active musicians who play in different constellations ranging from small bands to big orchestras with various music genres. We hope that you too will share your passion for music with others. So no matter what instrument you want to learn to play, we will do everything we can to help you in your development!

Learn, play and create. It's not only important to learn music but also putting it to good use. Except for the one-to-one lesson, we always offer our student a place in an ensemble. It could be a band or an orchestra that suits the students interest. Those who attend our music production and Sing & songwriter course will be creating music but also in instrument learning you need to be creative, and we will give you the tools.

So get into the music!
